Hand Cricket with Computer

Rules

  • Played between a player and the computer in an over (6 balls), one of you will either be the batter or the bowler in the first innings.
  • It starts with a toss: 'Heads or Tails'. If you win the toss, you can choose to either bat or bowl, otherwise, the computer will decide.
  • For example, if you win the toss and choose to bat, you can enter a number between 1 and 6. If your number matches the computer’s number, you are out! The computer will then have to chase your score,and vice versa!
  • If all 6 balls are bowled, the second innings will begin.
  • Now that you’ve understood the game, let’s play! Click the 'Start Game' button."
